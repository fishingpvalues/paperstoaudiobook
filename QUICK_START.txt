================================================================================
                   PDF TO AUDIOBOOK - QUICK START GUIDE
================================================================================

FOR YOUR SISTER - SUPER EASY TO USE!

STEP 1: INSTALL (First time only)
----------------------------------
1. Make sure Python is installed (python.org)
2. Open terminal/command prompt in this folder
3. Run: pip install -r requirements.txt

   OR just double-click: run.bat (Windows) or run.sh (Mac/Linux)
   It will install everything automatically!


STEP 2: ADD YOUR PDFs
----------------------
1. Open the "input" folder
2. Copy/paste your PDF study materials into it
3. That's it!


STEP 3: CONVERT TO AUDIOBOOKS
------------------------------
Double-click: run.bat (Windows) or run.sh (Mac/Linux)

OR in terminal, run: python pdf_to_audiobook.py


STEP 4: GET YOUR AUDIOBOOKS
----------------------------
1. Open the "output" folder
2. Your MP3 audiobooks are ready!
3. Copy to your phone, tablet, or listen on computer


================================================================================
                              THAT'S IT!
================================================================================

TIPS:
- One PDF = One MP3 audiobook
- Works with most scientific papers and study materials
- Voice is natural and pleasant for long listening
- Completely runs on your computer (no internet needed after setup)
- Processes multiple PDFs at once

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- If text sounds weird, your PDF might be scanned images
- Install Tesseract OCR for scanned PDFs (see README.md)
- Try one PDF first to test
